For AutoLoan-India Website

Last Updated: 06 March 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to AutoLoan-India. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website and submit details for auto loan assistance.

By using this website, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our website, we may collect the following information:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Vehicle details
  • Loan amount required
  • Location
  • IP address and browser information

This information is collected when you fill out forms or contact us for loan services.

3. Purpose of Collecting Information

We collect your information for the following purposes:

  • To connect you with banks or financial institutions for auto loans

  • To improve our services
  • To prevent fraud and misuse of our services

Your information may be shared with:

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

Our website may use cookies to:

Our website may contain links to third-party websites such as banks or loan providers. We are not responsible for their privacy policies or practices.

8. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Ask how your data is used

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
